ONE CLUB MANY CULTURES!

Welcome to RYAN sports club. At RYAN Sports Club, an effort has been made to provide world-class facilities and infrastructure for the promotion of sports and health.

During the past few years, RYAN Sports Club has emerged as one of the best facilities in the western suburbs of karachi in the area of sports and health and is making strides to acquire a formidable presence in the international arena also.

The club has a swimming pool of international size and standards, a fully equipped health club six acres of open ground for promoting outdoor sports like cricket, football, basket ball, tennis etc. and has facilities for indoor games like badminton, squash, billiards, snooker, table tennis, chess, carroms and bridge.
