Badminton

If you're looking for a place that is the breeding ground when it comes to promoting young and upcoming Badminton talent in the city, then this is the place to be.

Badminton is a great racquet sport that offers a tremendous amount of benefits. Whether its in singles or doubles, badminton is a fun and exciting sport to pick up if you are looking for an activity that boosts your physical and mental health.

The club has three state of the art badminton courts. The facility is capable of holding elite badminton tounaments. Qualified and experienced coaches and training arrangements are also available.

Famous Player

Crolina Marin

After winning the Women's Single World Championship in 2014 and 2015 Carolina is the number one female badminton player as of now. She is from Spain and is the first Spanish player to become the world No, 1 in the women's singles. Recently she won a gold medal in Olympics by defeating PV Sindhu of India. She has a powerful smash and moves swifter than anyone else around the court. In world rankings, she is the only left-handed player. Marin's mental strength and her fight back spirit amaze everyone time and again.

Rules

As outlined by the Badminton World Federation, here is a simplified rundown of the rules of badminton:

  • All singles and double matches are the best-of-three games. The first side to 21 points wins the game.
  • A point is scored on every serve and awarded to whichever side wins the rally.The side gets the next serve.
  • If the score is 20-20, a side must win by two clear points to win the game.If it reaches 29-29, the first to get to 30th point win.
