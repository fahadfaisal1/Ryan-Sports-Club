Rules

Here is a simplified rundown of the rules of tabletennis:

  • Games are played to 11 points.
  • Alternate serves every two points.
  • Toss the ball straight up when servin.
  • The serve can land anywhere in singles.
  • Double serves must go right court to right court.
  • A serve that touches the net in the way over is a 'let'
  • Alternate hitting in a doubles rally
  • Volles are not allowed
  • If your hit bounces back over the net its your point.
  • Touching the ball with your paddled hand is allowed
