Swimming

What better way to cool down than by having a swim. And if really want a well-toned body, then all you got to do is make a splash in the pool

The Club has the facility of covered and outdoor swimming pools.Special attention is paid to the maintenance of each pool in order to keep the water hygienically pure and hazard free. Uniform water temperature is maintained to meet the comfort level of the members. Professional life guards and coaching facility for both ladies and gents are also available.

Pool Facilities

COVERED POOL
The covered pool has the following specification:-

  • Pool depth deep end: 6 feet
  • Shallow end: 3.5 feet
  • Length: 25 meters
  • Width: 5 meters

OPEN POOL
The open pool has the following specification:-

  • Pool depth deep end: 8 feet
  • Shallow end: 4 feet
  • Length : 50 meters
  • Width: 21 meters
