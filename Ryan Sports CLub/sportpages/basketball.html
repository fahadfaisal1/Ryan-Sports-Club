Basketball

There's only one place to dribble and shoot the ball into the ring. Basketball's always been a fun game.

A basketball court is provided to entertain basketball lovers. The dimensions of the court are according to the international standard court measuring 94 feet by 50 feet. The club provides a wooden court with highly polished and fine maple wood.

Famous Player

Kareem Abdul Jabbar

While Kareem put up a whopping 38,387 points during his playing days, I can't look past the fact that he spent a good chunk of his career receiving passes from Oscar Robertson and Magic Johnson, the two greatest point guards of all time. Also, his career totals were inflated by the fact that he played roughly 10,000 years in the NBA. (Or 20. Whatever.) Nevertheless, he was an awesome force who dominated the sport for two decades and perfected the sky hook, one of the most gorgeous shots the game has ever seen. Plus, he was hilarious in Airplane! and fought Bruce Lee in Game of Death, so his cool quotient is easily the highest of anyone on this list.

Rules

As outlined by the National Basketball Association, here is a simplified rundown of the rules of basketball:

  • Only five players per team on the court.
  • Score more than your opponent to win.
  • Score within the shot clock.
  • Dribbling advances the ball.
  • The offense has five seconds to inbound the ball.
  • The offense must advance the ball.
  • Ball and ballhandler must remain inbounds.
  • Defenders can't interfere with a shot on a downward trajectory.
  • Defenders can legally block or steal the ball.
  • Defenders must leave the paint after three seconds
