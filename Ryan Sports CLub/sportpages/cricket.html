Famous Player

Imran Khan

The current Prime Minister of Pakistan, Imran Khan is also the founder and chairman of the political party Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf. Before making an entry into politics, Imran Khan was an extremely popular international cricketer who led Pakistan to its first ever Cricket World Cup title in 1992. As a philanthropist, he established Pakistan's first cancer hospital in Lahore.

Rules

As outlined by the international Cricket Council, here is a simplified rundown of the rules of cricket:

  • In cricket, there are always two teams and 22 players
  • Ruling of the umpire is final
  • Every six balls make an over
  • Duration of the game is negotiated
  • Professional cricket matches are fixed duration games
  • Batsman and bat both run for an over
  • When the ball hits the fence of the boundary, it means four runs
  • Overthrows could bring extra runs
  • Wasting any time on the field is penalized
  • Field placement is an optional call
